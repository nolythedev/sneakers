Nike Air Jordan

Air Jordan 1 Low SE 'Ceramic'

The Air Jordan 1 Low SE 'Ceramic' applies refined earth tones to a low-profile silhouette modeled after the 1985 original. The upper combines an off-white canvas base with orange suede overlays and an olive green suede Swoosh. The latter hue makes repeat appearances on the interior collar and the Jumpman logo embroidered on the tongue. A second Jumpman icon is stamped on the insole, while stitched Wings branding decorates the heel overlay. Underfoot, a gum rubber outsole provides sticky traction.
